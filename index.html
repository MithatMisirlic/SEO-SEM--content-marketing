Why HydroNutri?

Many middle school students face stress and mental fatigue due to poor hydration and nutrition. HydroNutri Hub tackles this by combining scientific insights with engaging comic-style storytelling and gamified learning experiences.

Our goal is to empower students with knowledge about how proper water intake and balanced diets directly impact cognitive function and mood. Through interactive e-learning modules, we transform essential habits into an exciting journey.
